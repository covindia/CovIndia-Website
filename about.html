About Us

We are a dedicated team of students and professors from Mahindra Ecole Centrale who are committed to keeping everyone as informed as we possibly can. We do not make any profit from this and hence are totally commited to not spreading false news. This is why ALL OUR UPDATES are backed by news articles from trustworthy sources like :

  • Ministry of Health and Family Welfare
  • Directorate of Health Services, Kerala
  • Livemint
  • Deccan Herald
  • Economic Times
  • The Hindu
